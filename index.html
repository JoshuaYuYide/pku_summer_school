Journey of the Summer School

From the participants' registration to the closing ceremony, over the course of one month, we have shared countless wonderful moments together.

Participants' registration

13, July

Offline registration of the summer school launch: Welcome 101 students from around the world to study at Peking University.

Opening Ceremony

14, July

The summer school was officially launched. Professor Song, Secretary of the Party Committee of the College of Advanced Manufacturing and Robotics, delivered a speech, and Professor You presided over the event. The participants officially started their studies at the summer school.

Foundations of Reinforcement Learning

14 - 25, July (9:00-11:00 UTC+8)

Professor Mallada explained the basic knowledge and algorithms of reinforcement learning to everyone.

Optimization Theory and Algorithms

14 - 18, July (13:00-17:00 UTC+8)

Professor Ling taught optimization-related theories and their applications in AI.

Learning-based Safe Control under Uncertainty

21 - 25, July (13:00-16:00 UTC+8)

Professor Gao introduced the theories and applications of robust and safe control.

Social Hour

18, July (15:00-17:00 UTC+8)

The faculty, students of the summer school, teachers from Peking University, and sponsors engaged in academic discussions and enjoyed the tea break together.

Closing Ceremony

25, July (16:00-16:30 UTC+8)

The summer school officially came to an end. Professor Li, Vice Dean of the College of Advanced Manufacturing and Robotics, delivered a speech, and Professor You presided over the event. Additionally, the summer school organizing committee awarded the title of "Outstanding Participant" to 12 participants.
